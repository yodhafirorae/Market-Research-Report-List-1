<p><p>The 6-axis welding robot market is primarily categorized into two types: spot welding robots and arc welding robots. Spot welding robots are designed for joining metal parts through localized heating and pressure, commonly used in the automotive industry for assembling components. Arc welding robots utilize a continuous arc to fuse materials, offering flexibility for various welding techniques and applications. Both types enhance production efficiency, precision, and safety, making them essential in modern manufacturing processes.</p></p>
