<p><strong>The Air Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Turbine Starter</li><li>Vane Starter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air starter market consists primarily of two types: turbine starters and vane starters. Turbine starters utilize a turbine mechanism powered by compressed air, which provides a high torque output, making them ideal for large engines and heavy-duty applications. In contrast, vane starters feature a rotating assembly with flexible vanes, allowing for smoother operation at lower speeds. These starters are typically lighter and suited for smaller engines. Both types offer advantages in terms of reliability, efficiency, and maintenance in various industries.</p></p>
