<p><p>The Aircraft Passenger Boarding Stairs market is categorized into two main types: towered and self-propelled. Towered boarding stairs are fixed structures often used at airports, providing a stable platform for passengers to embark and disembark from the aircraft. In contrast, self-propelled boarding stairs are mobile units that can easily maneuver around the airport, allowing quick access to various aircraft types without the need for ground support. Both types enhance operational efficiency and passenger convenience during boarding processes.</p></p>
