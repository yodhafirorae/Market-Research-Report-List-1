<p><p>The Applicant Tracking Software (ATS) market is divided into two main types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises ATS is installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control over data security and customization but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based ATS is hosted on remote servers, providing easier access, scalability, and automatic updates with lower upfront costs. Both types help organizations streamline recruitment processes, but they cater to different operational needs and resource capacities.</p></p>
