<p><strong>The Automotive Optical Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spherical Lens</li><li>Aspherical Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive optical lens market primarily features two types: spherical lenses and aspherical lenses. Spherical lenses have a constant curvature, providing uniform focus but may suffer from optical distortions and aberrations. In contrast, aspherical lenses boast a variable curvature, allowing for improved image quality, reduced aberrations, and enhanced light transmission. As a result, aspherical lenses are increasingly preferred for advanced automotive applications, such as headlights and camera systems, where precision and performance are critical in modern vehicle design.</p></p>
