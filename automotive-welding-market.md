<p><strong>The Automotive Welding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inert Protect Type</li><li>Semi-Inert Gas Protect Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive welding market features various types, including Inert Protect Type and Semi-Inert Gas Protect Type. Inert Protect Type welding utilizes gases like argon to create an oxygen-free environment, minimizing contamination and ensuring high-quality welds. Conversely, Semi-Inert Gas Protect Type uses a mix of inert and active gases, enhancing weld pool stability and penetration while still providing some level of protection against oxidation. Both types are critical for achieving durable, reliable welds in automotive manufacturing and repair.</p></p>
