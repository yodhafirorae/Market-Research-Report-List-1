<p><strong>The Built-in Oil Cooler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tube Coolers</li><li>Finned Coolers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The built-in oil cooler market features primarily two types: tube coolers and finned coolers. Tube coolers utilize a series of tubes to circulate oil, allowing efficient heat transfer and compact design, making them ideal for limited space applications. Finned coolers, on the other hand, incorporate extended surface areas, typically with metal fins, to enhance air contact and improve cooling efficiency. Both types cater to various industrial applications, ensuring optimal engine performance and longevity by maintaining operational temperatures.</p></p>
