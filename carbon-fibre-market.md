<p><p>The carbon fiber market can be categorized into two main types: composite and non-composite markets. The composite market primarily involves the use of carbon fiber reinforced polymers (CFRP), which are widely utilized in aerospace, automotive, and sporting goods due to their high strength-to-weight ratio and durability.
