<p><p>The continuous fiberglass yarn market is segmented into two main types: fine yarn and roving. Fine yarn, measuring less than 10 µm, is characterized by its high tensile strength and flexibility, making it suitable for applications that require precision and lightweight solutions, such as in aerospace and automotive industries. Roving, with a diameter of 10 µm and above, consists of untwisted strands, offering enhanced bulk and durability, ideal for reinforcement in composites and construction materials. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
