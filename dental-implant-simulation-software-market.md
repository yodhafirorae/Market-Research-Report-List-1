<p><p>Dental implant simulation software encompasses various types, primarily 3D viewing and DICOM viewing. 3D viewing allows dental professionals to visualize implant placements and anatomical structures in three-dimensional space, enhancing planning and decision-making. DICOM viewing, on the other hand, focuses on importing and visualizing medical imaging data from devices like CT scanners, enabling accurate assessments of patients' bone structure and density. Together, these tools facilitate precise implant planning and contribute to improved patient outcomes in dental procedures.</p></p>
