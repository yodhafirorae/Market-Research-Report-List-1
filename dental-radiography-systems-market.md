<p><p>The dental radiography systems market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Ordinary Radiography Systems** use traditional X-ray technology for basic imaging, primarily for detecting cavities and assessing tooth and bone health. </p><p>2. **Panoramic Radiography Systems** create a single, wide view of the entire mouth, useful for treatment planning and orthodontics. </p><p>3. **Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT)** provides 3D imaging, offering detailed views of dental structures, facilitating precise diagnostics and surgical planning. Each type serves specific diagnostic needs in dentistry.</p></p>
