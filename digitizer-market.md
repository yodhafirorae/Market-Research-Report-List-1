<p><p>The digitizer market can be categorized based on sampling resolution levels, notably 1024 Level and 2048 Level digitizers. The 1024 Level digitizers provide a resolution of 10 bits, enabling 1,024 discrete voltage levels for signal processing, making them suitable for basic applications. In contrast, 2048 Level digitizers, offering 11 bits of resolution, allow for finer signal detail with 2,048 discrete levels, ideal for more complex and high-fidelity applications. Other market types may include variations in resolution or specific application-focused digitizers.</p></p>
