<p><p>EHV XLPE power cables are categorized based on their voltage ratings. The 66-132 kV grade is commonly used for medium-range transmission, suitable for urban distribution. The up to 220 kV grade is utilized for regional transmission, connecting substations. The up to 400 kV grade is used for long-distance transmission of bulk power, essential in high-demand areas.
