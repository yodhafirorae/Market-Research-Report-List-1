<p><p>The electrical switchgear market is categorized into three main types based on voltage levels. High Voltage Switchgear operates in systems above 36 kV, used for electricity distribution in substations and large industrial applications. Medium Voltage Switchgear, ranging from 1 kV to 36 kV, serves in commercial and industrial facilities for equipment protection and control. Low Voltage Switchgear, utilized below 1 kV, is designed for residential and small commercial applications, ensuring safe and efficient electrical distribution and circuit protection.</p></p>
