<p><p>The fluorinated fluids market comprises three primary types: lubricants, solvents, and heat transfer fluids. Fluorinated lubricants are designed for extreme conditions and provide excellent thermal stability and low friction properties, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Solvents in this category offer high solvency power and are non-flammable, making them suitable for cleaning and extraction processes. Heat transfer fluids are utilized in various industrial applications due to their superior thermal conductivity and stability, enabling efficient heat management in challenging environments.</p></p>
