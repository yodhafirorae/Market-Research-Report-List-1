<p><p>The Gunshot Detection System market is primarily categorized into two types: Acoustic Gunshot Detection Systems and Optical Gunshot Detection Systems. Acoustic systems utilize advanced microphones to capture the sound of gunfire, analyzing audio waves to pinpoint gunshot locations. In contrast, Optical systems rely on visual sensors and cameras to detect the flash or heat signature produced by a gunshot. Both technologies enhance public safety and security by providing rapid, accurate location data for law enforcement and emergency responders.</p></p>
