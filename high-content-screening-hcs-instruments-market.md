<p><p>The High Content Screening (HCS) instruments market comprises two primary types: cell imaging and analysis systems, and flow cytometers. Cell imaging and analysis systems utilize advanced microscopy and software to assess cellular parameters, enabling the study of cellular responses to various stimuli at a high throughput. Flow cytometers, on the other hand, analyze and quantify physical and chemical characteristics of cells or particles in a fluid stream, facilitating rapid multiparametric analysis. Both play crucial roles in drug discovery, toxicology, and cellular biology research.</p></p>
