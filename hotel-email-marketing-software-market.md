<p><p>Hotel email marketing software is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, allowing hotels to access it from anywhere with an internet connection. This type often features subscription pricing and automatic updates. On-premises solutions involve installing software directly on a hotel's local servers, offering greater data control and customization but requiring more IT resources and maintenance. Each type caters to different operational needs and budget considerations in the hospitality industry.</p></p>
