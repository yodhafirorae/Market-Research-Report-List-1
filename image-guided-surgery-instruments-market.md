<p><p>The image-guided surgery instruments market encompasses various advanced imaging technologies that aid surgeons in performing precise procedures. Computed Tomography (CT) provides detailed cross-sectional images, while Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) offers high-resolution soft tissue contrast. X-Ray Fluoroscopy allows real-time imaging during surgery. Endoscopes enable visualization of internal structures through minimally invasive techniques. Position Emission Tomography (PET) and Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) provide functional imaging, helping to assess metabolic activity and guide surgical intervention, enhancing accuracy and outcomes.</p></p>
