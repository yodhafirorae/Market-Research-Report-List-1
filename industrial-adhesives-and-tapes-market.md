<p><p>The industrial adhesives and tapes market comprises various types each tailored for specific applications. Hot melt adhesives provide quick bonding through heat application, ideal for packaging and assembly. Contact spray adhesives offer immediate bonding once surfaces touch, effective for laminating materials. Acrylic adhesives are versatile, known for their clarity and strength. Anaerobic adhesives cure in the absence of air, commonly used in metal bonding. Epoxy adhesives deliver high strength and resistance, suitable for industrial applications. Other adhesives include specialty formulations for unique requirements.</p></p>
