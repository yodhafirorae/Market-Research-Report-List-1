<p><p>The Lyocell fiber market consists primarily of two types: Regular Lyocell Fiber and Crosslinked Lyocell Fiber. Regular Lyocell Fiber is known for its exceptional softness, breathability, and moisture-wicking properties, making it suitable for apparel and home textiles. In contrast, Crosslinked Lyocell Fiber undergoes a chemical modification process that enhances durability and resistance to wrinkling, making it ideal for products that require greater stability and longevity, such as industrial textiles and specialty applications. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
