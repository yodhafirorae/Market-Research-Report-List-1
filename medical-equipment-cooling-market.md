<p><p>The Medical Equipment Cooling Market comprises two primary types: liquid-based cooling and air-based cooling. Liquid-based cooling utilizes fluids to dissipate heat, offering efficient thermal management for sensitive medical devices, such as MRI machines and lasers. Conversely, air-based cooling relies on air circulation to maintain optimal temperatures, commonly seen in smaller equipment and environments. Both cooling methods are critical for enhancing the performance and lifespan of medical devices, ensuring patient safety and accuracy in clinical settings.</p></p>
