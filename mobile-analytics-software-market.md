<p><p>Mobile analytics software is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based analytics leverage remote servers to store and process data, enabling users to access insights from anywhere with internet connectivity. This model typically offers scalability and lower upfront costs. In contrast, on-premises solutions require installation on local servers, providing better control over data security and customization but often entail higher maintenance costs and resource requirements. Both types cater to different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
