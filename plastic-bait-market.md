<p><p>The plastic bait market is primarily divided into two types: hard bait and soft bait. Hard baits are typically made from rigid plastics and are designed to mimic the movement and appearance of various aquatic creatures, making them effective for attracting larger fish. Soft baits, on the other hand, are flexible and often resemble worms, grubs, or other prey, making them appealing for a variety of fishing techniques. Both types cater to different fishing preferences and species, enhancing the overall angling experience.</p></p>
