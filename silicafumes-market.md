<p><ul><li>Densified Silica Fume</li><li>Semi Densified Silica Fume</li><li>Undensified Silica Fume</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicafumes market is categorized into three types: densified, semi-densified, and undensified silica fume. Densified silica fume features compacted particles for easier handling and improved flow properties, making it suitable for high-performance concrete applications. Semi-densified silica fume offers a balance between performance and cost, providing moderate flowability and applicability. Undensified silica fume, the least processed, is used in various industrial applications where cost-effectiveness and raw material properties are prioritized, typically resulting in higher water demand in mixtures.</p></p>
