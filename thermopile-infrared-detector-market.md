<p><p>The Thermopile Infrared Detector market features two main types: Through Hole Thermopile Infrared Sensors and SMD (Surface-Mount Device) Thermopile Infrared Sensors. Through Hole sensors are designed for traditional printed circuit board (PCB) mounting, offering robust performance in various applications. In contrast, SMD sensors provide a compact footprint, enabling efficient assembly and improved integration in modern electronic devices. Both types are crucial for applications such as temperature sensing, gas analysis, and motion detection, catering to diverse industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
