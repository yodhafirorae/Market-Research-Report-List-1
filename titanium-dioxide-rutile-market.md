<p><p>The Titanium Dioxide Rutile market is primarily segmented into two production processes: the Sulphate Process and the Chlorination Process. The Sulphate Process involves converting titanium ore into titanium dioxide using sulfuric acid, producing a lower-quality product suitable for various applications, including paints and coatings. Conversely, the Chlorination Process utilizes chlorine and is more efficient, yielding a higher purity titanium dioxide, ideal for specialized applications like cosmetics and high-performance coatings. Both processes cater to the diverse demands of the market.</p></p>
