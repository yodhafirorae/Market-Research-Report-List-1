<p><p>The vascular injury treatment market includes various techniques addressing injured blood vessels. Lateral arteriorrhaphy/venorrhaphy involves suturing the edges of a vessel to repair it. Patch angioplasty uses a graft material to widen narrowed vessels. Bypass graft creates a detour around a blockage using a segment of another vessel. Extraanatomic bypass reroutes blood flow outside the affected area. Thrombectomy involves clot removal to restore blood flow. Additionally, other specialized methods cater to diverse vascular injury scenarios.</p></p>
