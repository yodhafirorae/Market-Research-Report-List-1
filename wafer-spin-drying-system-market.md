<p><p>The Wafer Spin Drying System market encompasses two primary types: Single Stack Systems and Dual Stack Systems. Single Stack Systems are designed to process wafers in a single layer, optimizing space efficiency and cost-effectiveness for smaller production demands. In contrast, Dual Stack Systems allow for the simultaneous processing of multiple wafer stacks, significantly enhancing throughput and productivity for high-volume manufacturing environments. Both systems are essential in semiconductor fabrication, ensuring effective moisture removal and preparing wafers for subsequent processing steps.</p></p>
