<p><p>The waterproofing membrane market comprises several types, with modified bitumen membranes and synthetic polymer waterproofing membranes being prominent. Modified bitumen membranes combine asphalt with polymer additives for enhanced flexibility and resistance to aging, making them ideal for flat roofs and varying weather conditions. In contrast, synthetic polymer waterproofing membranes, such as PVC and TPO, offer superior UV resistance and longevity, suitable for a wide range of applications including roofs, walls, and foundations. Both types provide essential protection against water infiltration and damage.</p></p>
