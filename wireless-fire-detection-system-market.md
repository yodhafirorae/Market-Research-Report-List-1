<p><p>The wireless fire detection system market features two main types: fully wireless systems and hybrid systems. Fully wireless systems operate entirely on battery power and wireless communication, offering easy installation and flexibility, making them ideal for retrofitting in existing structures. Hybrid systems combine wired and wireless technologies, providing a reliable solution with the benefits of both types, often using wired connections for primary devices while incorporating wireless sensors for extended coverage. Both types enhance fire safety with advanced detection capabilities.</p></p>
