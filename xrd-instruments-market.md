<p><p>The XRD instruments market encompasses two primary types: Powder XRD and Single-Crystal XRD. Powder XRD is commonly used for analyzing crystalline materials by examining the diffraction patterns of powdered samples, making it ideal for phase identification and quantification. In contrast, Single-Crystal XRD focuses on analyzing individual crystals, providing detailed information about the crystal structure, lattice parameters, and molecular arrangement. Each type serves distinct applications across various industries, including materials science, chemistry, and pharmaceuticals, driving their respective market growth.</p></p>
